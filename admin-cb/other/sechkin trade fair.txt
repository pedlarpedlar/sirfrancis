About and Information:

The Seçhkin Trade Fair and Convention stands out as South Africa's most expansive and impactful career guidance initiative, actively endorsed and supported by the quadruple helix. Specifically addressing the pressing issue of youth unemployment, this outdoor trade fair and business convention takes center stage in the Eastern Cape, a region emblematic of poverty and joblessness in the country.

Distinguished as the most comprehensive and innovative event, the Seçhkin Trade Fair and Convention is meticulously crafted to empower and prepare learners, as well as professionals, for the dynamic world of work. By transforming essential components of a career guidance program into an interactive experience, the fair offers a meaningful platform for exploration to both youth and seasoned professionals.

This groundbreaking event introduces resources that include expert insights into opportunities within skill-shortage professions, details on bursaries and entrepreneurship, as well as seminars. The Career Guidance Workbook, a vital component, breathes life into South Africa's largest careers and skills event.

In collaboration with dedicated partners, the Seçhkin Trade Fair and Convention emerges as a potent, resourceful, and comprehensive tool aimed at reaching and equipping South Africa's expanding population of school-leaving and unemployed youths, as well as providing a valuable platform for businesses of all sizes to book stands and come together.

Gondwana Café
0.12 km / Cafés & Eateries

Sandpiper Restaurant

The Groendal Wilderness Area protects the water catchment area of the Swartkops River, featuring the Groendal Dam and diverse ecosystems. Explore the wilderness.

Sardinia Bay Marine Protected Area, adjacent to the Sardinia Bay Nature Reserve, is a 7 km rocky and sandy coastline with diverse marine life. Discover the protected bay.

Kragga Kamma Game Park

Kragga Kamma Game Park, just a few hundred meters from the main road, offers an opportunity to see Africa’s wildest species in a natural setting. Explore the park.

iBhayi Sunshine Saunter Route
18.17 km / Entertainment
Port Elizabeth's version of the Midlands Meander, the iBhayi Sunshine Saunter Route, is a circular tourist route aimed at both locals and visitors. Discover local attractions and experiences.
